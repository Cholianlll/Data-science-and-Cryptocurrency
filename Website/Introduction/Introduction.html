Introduction


Since 2009, the first decentralized cryptocurrency, bitcoin, was created by presumably pseudonymous developer Satoshi Nakamoto, “Crypto” related things have grown into a public-hitting topic. With a few years” explosion in this field, a lot of derived tokens have come out, such as “Ethereum”, “Litecoin”, and even “Dogecoin”. Counted by the exchange “Binance”, there are 1663 tokens publicly traded now.


What is this project doing?


Since Cryptocurrency has come to a hit to the whole world, many people are curious about a key question: what decides the cryptocurrency value? A popular answer is "Common sense", which is more like the public confidence in something. One of the manifestations is what kind of topic the public is talking about now.


This website will provide detailed information about various tokens including mainstream BTC, ETH, Litecoin Dogecoin, and other cryptocurrencies.


Beyond that, this website will explore more exciting things. Especially in the relationship between the twitter content and cryptocurrencies.

Task 1: Public topics


“Crypto” related things have grown into a public-hitting topic. With a few years' explosion in this area, the traditional crypto topic, concentrated in discussing market quotes and pure fanaticism, has derived into several different directions, including “NFT”, “Bitcoins”, “Ethereum”, “Defi”, “Metaverse” and so on.


The first task of this project is aimed to explore some insights hidden in the massive topic squares platform, such as the Twitter platform.


The first and most important thing is to explore whether there are any gaps within this huge area, implying a trend from a single topic to diversified areas. Then, for every single sub-topic, what special theme is will be the second task. Finally, after building several successful models for detecting and exploring different topics, the final stage is aimed to build the auto-enlabeling system on each new tweet, classifying each tweet into the right sub-topic.


Task 2: Market quotes


Currentlt, the total market size of over 10,000 cryptocurrencies in the world has reached over $2.8 trillion(Sandeep, 2021) while the total market cap of gold is only around 11 trillion . Apparently, it has been grown into a huge market, and thousands of people are attracted to invest in this field.


This project is aimed to explore some hidden patterns behind this emerging market with data science insights. There will be several expected contributions that came from this project.


Firstly, since the market size has been enough large for some mechain learning techniques to be implemented on it, our work can be acted as a pioneer or a good example of guiding other people who are interested in this field. Secondly, there are lots of unknown questions or explanations on the future of this field. By trying to explain some abnormal phenomenon with professional data science knowledge, it may bring new insight and even bring the answer behind the market.
